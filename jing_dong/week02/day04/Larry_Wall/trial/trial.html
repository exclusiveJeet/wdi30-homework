Who is Larry Wall

Larry Wall, programmer, linguist, author, born March 10, 1949 in Duncan, British Columbia, Canada. Created 1987 the computer language Perl

Larry Wall is the computer programmer responsible for creating Perl, a powerful general-purpose programming language known for its strengths in text processing. Wall, whose graduate work was in linguistics, designed Perl in 1987 for reports processing and continues to oversee the language's development according to the motto "Larry is always right, even when he was wrong."
