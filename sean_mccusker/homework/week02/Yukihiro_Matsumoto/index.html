Biography

Yukishiro Matsumoto is a Japanese computer scientist, as well as, a software programmer. He is the father of Ruby programming language.

Matsumoto used to be the chief head architect of Ruby at Heroku. He is also a fellow of Rakuten Institute of Technology. Additinoally, he was appointed to the role of technical advisor for VASILY, Inc. in June of 2014
