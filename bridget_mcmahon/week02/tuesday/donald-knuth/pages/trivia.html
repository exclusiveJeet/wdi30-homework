Trivia

Chinese Name

Knuth's Chinese name is Gao Dena (高德纳). He was given this name by Frances Yeo in 1977, before a 3 week trip to China. Knuth embraced this name because he wanted to be recognised by the growing number of computer programmers in China.

Computer Scientist Humour

Until 2008, Knuth would pay anyone who could find typographical errors or mistakes in his books $2.56, because "256 pennies is one hexadecimal dollar". These cheques were considered by many to be among computerdom's most prized trophies.
